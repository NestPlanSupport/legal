Frequently Asked Questions

How can I invite my partner?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Manage Family ‚Üí "Generate Invite Code". Share the code with your partner, who can enter it under "Join Family".
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí "Delete Account". All your data will be permanently deleted.
Can I use NestPlan on multiple devices?
Yes, simply sign in with your account on another device. All data will be automatically synchronized.
How can I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions on your iPhone.
Is my data secure?
Yes, sensitive data such as health information is stored encrypted. Only you and your family have access.
